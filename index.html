My Journey

From Civil
Blueprints to
Digital Interfaces

I began with a B.Tech in Civil Engineering, learning to build structures that must hold under pressure. But behind the calculations, I found myself drawn to a different kind of design — one shaped by people, not concrete. Fascinated by how digital experiences remove friction and create real impact, I made the leap into UI/UX in 2021.

My engineering precision became my design superpower. I channelled that discipline into crafting intuitive interfaces at Hexaware Technologies, then deepened my expertise with a Master's in Computer Science and Management at Avila University — merging design thinking with technology and strategy.

Today, I design at scale for Cisco Systems, building products used by millions. Every interaction I design is grounded in empathy, tested with rigor, and refined with care.

Civil engineering taught me to design for how people move through space. UI/UX let me take that instinct digital — where every click, every scroll, every interaction becomes a path worth perfecting.
